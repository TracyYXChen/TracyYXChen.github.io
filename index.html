About


I'm Yuexi Chen (陈玥西). I also go by Tracy because it sounds like my Chinese name. Currently I'm pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park (Expected 2022). I'm co-advised by Prof. Max Leiserson and Prof. David Fushman.
